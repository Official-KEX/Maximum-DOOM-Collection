Title                   : Return to Castle Wolfenstein (doom2 ver)  
Filename                : r2wolfII.zip
Author                  : Norman Scott/Tim Scott
Email Address           : tscott@plains.nodak.edu

Description             : Replaces first 10 maps of Doom][ with
                          new maps.  This is a revision of the doom1
                          Episode 1 replacement. The maps contain 
                          some levels previously released by
                          authors, modified levels, and completely
                          new levels.   Contains characters and
                          textures from Wolfenstein.  There is 
                          also a new weapon to add to your pack.

Additional Credits to   * The creators of Wolfenstein
                        * The creators of Doom
                        * The creator of Deusf to handle all the sprites.
                        * Bill Neisius for his audio and graphic
                          editors for both Doom and Wolfenstein
                        * The creator dehacked2.0
                        * The creators of Trinity for use of graphics
