Misc. Author Info       : Author of the CRINGE! episode for

                          MKMINES and MKHALLS.

Your Mission            : Shoot things and find stuff. Avoid junk food.
                          Be aware that there is an instant death teleporter
                          that will instantly kill all players when used.
                          This does not work in Deathmatch.
                          There is also a modified COLORMAP and PLAYPAL
                          in this level.  There is one sector that is safe
                          from all monsters.  Can you find it?

Additional Credits to   : Peter Sigler & Eric Sargent for their time 
                          testing Multi-Player modes.  
                          Special thanks to everyone who plays 
                          this level and likes it.

ATTENTION!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!: Many WAD users, like myself, sometimes load the
                          WADs that they download into their level editors
                          and run checks on them to make sure they are okay.
                          If you run a check on MKFRGTME.WAD, you will get
                          a bunch of MISSING TEXTURE warnings, due to the
                          fact that there are no lower or upper textures
                          on some of the sidedefs.  The level was 
                          intentionally designed that way in order to 
                          achieve various special effects.  If you have your
                          editor to fix the missing textures, then you will
                          have screwed up the level instantly.  Be assured
                          that ALL sectors are closed, and all cross-refs
                          are safe.  PLEASE DO NOT TRY TO PUT THE TEXTURES
                          WHERE I HAVE LEFT THEM OUT.  Thank you.
