DOOM 2

Part eleven of the RAVEN SERIES Map 11

This is part 11 in the premier Doom 2 RAVEN series.  
This .wad will challenge all Doom players regardless 
of skill level.

Also included is Raven 11b, a smaller level that
works great for deathmatch on slower machines.  If
Raven 11 is too slow, play Raven 11b.  Both wads
support single and deathmatch play.


Features:
- support for all 5 skill levels
- Great DEATHMATCH level 11 or 11b
- Great outdoor settings
- well placed monsters
- use of all three keys 11 only
- can be completed with out cheating
- excellent texture alignment
- great windows for sniping
- Tall buildings
- HINT: The bridge could be more than it looks.


Use doom2 -file raven11.wad -warp 11 -skill 1  
to play or idclev11   during the game. 
     
      ** OR  **

Use doom2 -file raven11b.wad -warp 11 -skill 1 
to play or idclev11   during the game.
