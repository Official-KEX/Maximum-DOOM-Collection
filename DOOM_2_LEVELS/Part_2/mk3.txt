Your Mission            : The CyberDemon Lord and his minions have slain
                          all of the miners and claimed the territory for
                          their own.  The demon carpenters have just started 
                          reconstructing the mines into the rich surroundings 
                          that the CyberDemon Lord enjoys the most.  It will
                          take two keys to end this madness and one to leave.
                          The mine shafts are now watched over by groups of
                          imps with their guard dog spectres, while the demons
                          get to do all the actual work.  Several stronger
                          creatures have been recruited as guardians of the 
                          keys.  Few former humans have been summoned here by 
                          the CyberDemon Lord as their services are not yet 
                          required; however, if the Lord feels any threat, 
                          like from you, then he is likely to dispatch them to 
                          his disposal.  Don't let nuthin bite cha!
