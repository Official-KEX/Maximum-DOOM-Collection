* Play Information *

Game                    : DOOM II
Level #                 : MAP1
Single Player           : Yes, just to take a look, I only put in 4 monsters. 
Cooperative 2-4 Player  : What's the point?
Deathmatch 2-4 Player   : YES! Kill yer buddies...very nice 2-3 player and
                          hectic 4 player.  There's a lot of action in the
                          room you teleport to, the one with all the ammo...
                          [There are several secret switches that open secret
                          doors that you should find before playing in a
                          Deathmatch.]  There is an exit behind two candles on 
                          the platform in the north room, this will give you
                          an official frag score at the end of play.  
                          Enjoy.  [woof]
Difficulty Settings     : Yes, [only for the monsters.]
